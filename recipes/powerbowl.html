Homemade Power Bowl

Description

Ingredients

Ingredients needed before proceeding.

  • Knorr Mexican or Spanish rice
  • Sour cream
  • Buttermilk
  • Avocados
  • Fresh garlic
  • Fresh lime juice
  • Dry ranch dressing mix
  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Red onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Canned black beans
  • Shredded Cheaddar cheese
  • Low fat sour cream
How to Make the Power Bowl
  1. Cook the rice according to the package directions
  2. Place sour cream, buttermilk, ripe avocado, chopped garlic, lime juice, and dry ramch dressing mix into a blender. Puree until smooth.
  3. Cook the chicken in a skillet or until cooked through. Then thinly slice the chicken.
  4. Shred the romaine lettuce.
  5. Run water over the beans to rinse them and drain off excess water.
  6. Wash and chop the tomatoes.
  7. Make guacamole by mashing the avocado in a bowl and stirring in lime juice, onions, tomatoes, and salt.
  8. Build the bowl with rice on the bottom then topping it with portions of chicken, black beans, guacamole, chopped tomatoes, low fat sour cream, cheese, and chopped lettuce.
  9. Drizzle avocado ranch sauce on top and serve immediately.
